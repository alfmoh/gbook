Connecting patients and medical organizations

GB - the novel blockchain-based platform that connects between patients, doctors, clinics, medical researches, Health Medical organizations (HMOs), health insurance and pharma companies and lead generators.

Be part of the innovation!

Communication between patients and medical organizations is broken!

Billions of patients, millions of clinics and doctors, dozens of thousand of insurers and research still use phone, faxes, paper and CD to communicate...

Patients:

  • Need to call clinics instead of online booking
  • Often don't have an instant access to their medical data

Clinics:

  • The lack of actual data on free timeslots to book a doctor results in lost patients

Doctors

  • No immediate access to all medical data

Researchers

  • No immediate access to all medical data

Insurers and HMOs

  • Can't share medical information instantly between different clinics
  • Communication with clinics and patients is mainly by phone

Lead generators

  • Lower lead conversation because of call-centers and human factors
  • The lack of option for 24x7 real-time medical appointment scheduling

The Solution

Blockchain-powered electronic health record (EHR) and appointment scheduling platform that connects all medical organization and patients. Any transaction between the parties shall involve GBooking token (“GB” token) exchange. It ensures security, access rights management and besides all – the token value growth, as it naturally grows together with the ecosystem enlargement.

The GB token could be mined using medical image processing algorithms to help clinics to render medical images. This alone makes GB very attractive to existing and new miners and therefore stimulates the ecosystem development.

We focus on:

  • Instant EHR data sharing
  • Real-time actions allowance
  • Security
  • Data immutability
  • Communication automation

Patients use health apps and medical peripherals to gain and share medical data

Researches and pharma companies get access to depersonalized medical information

Patients instantly share with clinic EHR/ EMR data with clinics and doctors

Insurance companies share analytics and statistic with pharma companies and researches

Clinics smoothly exchange data and contracts with insurers and HMOs

How GB chnages the medical world

  • Patients call the call-centers of Lead Generators (LG) sites, LG representatives call then clinics’ call-centers to get the actual schedule, LG then call patients back.
  • The enormous number of integrations is required between clinics and LG. This make it practically impossible to implement
  • The number of wasted leads is high, the processing costs are high either. No way to share EHR easily between different medical organizations
